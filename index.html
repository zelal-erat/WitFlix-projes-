DEJA VU

7.3 IMDB 16+ 2 h 21 min

Everyone has experienced the disturbing mystery of the event known as Deja Vu in some way. When you meet someone for the first time, you feel as if you have known them for years. Or when you go somewhere for the first time, you feel as if you have been there before. In short, it can be said that there is no one who does not know the feeling called Deja Vu. Well, what if this strange and creepy feeling is actually a warning sent from the past... Or if it contains clues from the unknown future... In the new action-thriller by producer Jerry Bruckheimer and director Tony Scott, deja vu begins.
